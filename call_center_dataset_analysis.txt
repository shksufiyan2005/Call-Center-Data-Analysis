
Task Description:

Participants are expected to analyze the call center dataset to identify patterns, trends, and actionable insights. 
They should focus on leveraging the available data to achieve the following specific objectives:

1. Agent Performance Evaluation:
   - Analyze individual agent performance based on metrics like resolution rate, average talk duration, and customer satisfaction ratings.
   - Identify top-performing agents and those who may need additional training or support.

2. Call Topic Insights:
   - Determine the distribution and frequency of different call topics.
   - Identify any trends or patterns in call topics over time.
   - Provide recommendations for training or resource allocation based on the prevalence of certain topics.

3. Service Level Optimization:
   - Assess the overall service level by analyzing metrics such as speed of answer, resolution rate, and customer satisfaction.
   - Identify peak hours and low-service periods to recommend staffing adjustments.

4. Objectives:

- Insight Generation: Uncover meaningful insights that can inform decision-making and strategy for the call center.
- Actionable Recommendations: Provide concrete, actionable recommendations based on data analysis to improve call center performance.

Participants should aim to present their findings and recommendations clearly and compellingly, demonstrating how their analysis can 
lead to tangible improvements in call center operations. The ultimate goal is to leverage data to enhance efficiency, 
improve customer satisfaction, and optimize resource allocation.
